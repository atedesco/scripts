CoRD Script Install from USB

1. Copy the folder 'cord' and 'usbcordcp' onto a Fluid USB stick.
2. Assure that the USB key is name as 'NO NAME'
3. Eject USB Key
4. Insert USB Key into team members Mac
5. Double Click 'NO NAME'
6. Double click 'usbcordcp'
7. Click 'Ok'
8. Have them login to Projecter-PSA
9. Eject USB key 'NO NAME'
